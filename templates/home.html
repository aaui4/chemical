About This Project

The Chemical Reaction Simulation Website is an educational platform designed to help students understand chemical reactions through interactive and visual simulations.

Project Goal

To simplify chemical concepts and make learning chemistry more engaging and accessible for students.

What You Can Do

Browse chemical reactions, explore equations, and simulate reactions by selecting reactants and conditions.

Academic Context

This project is developed as a university-level (L3) academic project combining chemistry and web development.
